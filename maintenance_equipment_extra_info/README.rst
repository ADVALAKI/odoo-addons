================================
Maintenance equipment extra info
================================

 * Lot/Serial Number, and purchase invoice line in equipment form.
 * Allow selecting invoice lines of purchase, whose product is equal to the
   product of the lot.
 * When selecting the purchase invoice line, display the following fields:
   supplier, invoice, invoice date, invoice status, base amount.
